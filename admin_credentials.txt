CCS-SITIN Monitoring System - Default Admin Credentials
===============================================

Username: admin
Password: admin

IMPORTANT: Please change these default credentials after your first login for security purposes.

To change your password:
1. Log in to the system
2. Navigate to your profile settings
3. Select "Change Password"
4. Enter the new password and confirm it

For security reasons, choose a strong password that:
- Is at least 8 characters long
- Contains a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Includes numbers and special characters
- Is not used for other accounts

Keep this information secure and do not share it with unauthorized personnel.
